About

The CNRL Open House 2026 is an opportunity to explore cutting-edge research at the Chiari Neurobiomechanics Research Laboratory at Northeastern University. Join us for a day of talks, poster presentations, and live demonstrations showcasing our work at the intersection of neuroscience, biomechanics, and computational modeling.

The event is open to students, faculty, clinicians, and researchers with an interest in Chiari malformation, cerebrospinal fluid dynamics, and related neurobiomechanical topics. No prior specialization is required — all are welcome.
